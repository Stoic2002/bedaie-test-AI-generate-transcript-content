## Design Context

### Users
Penilai tugas teknis (HR & Developer) serta content creator profesional. Mereka membutuhkan alat yang cepat, intuitif, bebas gangguan, dan efisien untuk pekerjaan atau evaluasi sehari-hari.

### Brand Personality
Clean, Minimalist, Professional. Desain yang mencerminkan rasa percaya (trustworthy), efisiensi, dan fungsionalitas murni tanpa gimmick.

### Aesthetic Direction
Modern Minimalist (Light Mode Only).
Referensi: Dashboard Vercel, Dokumentasi Next.js, Notion.
Anti-referensi: Desain ramai yang padat komponen, warna-warna norak (garish), Web SaaS/Korporat usang.
Fokus Utama: Tipografi yang rapi (Geist), pemanfaatan *whitespace* yang maksimal, dan fungsionalitas mirip Shadcn UI.

### Design Principles
1. **Speed & Clarity Above All**: Implement functional, fast, and highly intuitive interfaces. Avoid overwhelming the user by keeping layouts lean and focused.
2. **Whitespace over Containers**: Lean heavily on well-calibrated spacing to create layout hierarchy rather than boxing everything into heavy cards with borders (Notion/Vercel paradigm).
3. **Restrained but Premium Contrast**: Strictly use a professional light-mode aesthetic. Adhere to WCAG contrast standards ensuring that background and text pairs are highly legible.
4. **Purposeful Elements Only**: Every UI component must serve a functional need. No unnecessary ornamental graphics or complex layout structures. Clean lines only.
